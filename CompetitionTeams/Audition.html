**Guest Choreographers for Legato and Vivace advanced teams are still being planned and a seperate audition could be arranged for guest choreographers upon arrival to Allegro.

Signed contracts must be turned in on the audition day prior to auditioning.

Performers must show up to audition in dance attire and well-groomed. Plan on giving yourself a good warm-up.

Dancers are welcome to audition for the style(s) of dance that they choose. Please only audition for the dance pieces that you can give 100% of your effort to, it is okay not to audition for every piece. Once placements have been made dancers are committed to full participation of each dance.

The choreographers of each piece will choose dancers with the help of the other judges at the audition. Dancers will be chosen based on their strengths, style, performance ability and technical ability.
